Olympus Adventure Park

Olympus Adventure Park is a one-of-a-kind theme park inspired by the rich mythology and culture of Ancient Greece. This destination blends history, fun, and adventure to provide an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

Poseidon's Trident

"Fear Takers, Late Breakers, and History Makers"

  • Sharp-Turning, Fast Moving Trident (Is 150mph too much?)
  • Haven't Showered? We Got You! (Spoiler Alert: We are getting splashed!)
  • Discover True Fear Going in the Apathos Peak (Screams Allowed)

Security

  • Safety is our number 1 priority, including restraints and seatbelts to handle high speeds.
  • Not apt for people with medical issues like neck problems or heart conditions.
  • Be aware: consuming food before riding might increase discomfort!

Features

  • Fast acceleration from 0 to 150MPH in just seconds.
  • Topath Turn: Experience 4.9 G's on a sharp 85-degree curve.
  • Poseidon's Flight: An 80-degree uphill followed by a 130ft drop reaching top speeds.

Skylos

Our Beautiful Mascot

  • Skylos is based on Cerberus, the multi-headed dog guarding the gates of the underworld.

Why Skylos?

  • Skylos means "dog" in Greek, representing a family-friendly vibe for our park.
